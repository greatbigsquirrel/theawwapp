While this is currently an extension on chrome, keep your eyes peeled as it will very shortly be coming out on Samsung through the Tizen store and on the Android store!

What it does: as most subreddits host images in dedicated imgur folders, this app directly pulls the APIs for these folders and lets you browse just the images and GIFs. It pulls an array and provides these to you randomly, however you are able to go back in the list and see other pictures as you like. Options can be changed at any time, unless of course you turned the buttons off- however a quick refresh or just swiping backwards will take you to the homepage so you can change the options again.

The hammertime js swipes are not mine, I got them off another github account. If you want to be one of the cool kids and dig through the guts of the app yourself, please visit my GitHub, where you can see the various incarnations as well as the option to tinker.
